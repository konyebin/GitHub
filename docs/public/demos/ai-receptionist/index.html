Why · Handle objections

Objections — and how to respond

Address fears head-on. Customers buy confidence, not technology.

"It sounds too complex to set up."

AI Receptionist deploys in minutes inside Control Hub — no technical expertise required. Phone number, voice, knowledge base, routing rules. Done.

"Our callers won't like talking to AI."

Natural, conversational speech — not robotic menus. Callers ask questions normally and get transferred to a human when they need one.

"We already have an auto attendant."

Auto attendants route by button presses. AI Receptionist understands intent — "I need billing" vs. "What's your address?" — and routes intelligently.

"What about accuracy?"

You control the knowledge base — company info, FAQs, policies. AI answers from your content and escalates when it doesn't know.

We make AI simple and easy to use — lead with the 5-step wizard, not the underlying LLM.
